Anthony Marino

Tony Marino is a Los Angeles based entertainer specializing in sound design and music. Growing up in Massachusetts, he discovered his gift of perfect pitch at age five, and began taking piano lessons starting at age ten. Tony has trained under Grammy-award winning producers Michael Verdick and the late Roger Nichols, as well as interned under producer Kevin Augunas for artists such as The Lumineers. He enjoys all forms of music but has a special place in his heart for Japanese pop and rock after visiting the country in 2006; these influences are reflected in his own original works.

Tony's most recent role was as audio director for California based game developer Studio Roqovan, where he served (among many other things) as a sound designer on the upcoming virtual reality title World War Toons.

He has also previously worked at WayForward Technologies as a proud member of the audio team, contributing his sound design to numerous releases including:

  • DuckTales Remastered
  • Regular Show: Mordecai And Rigby In 8-Bit Land
  • Adventure Time: Explore the Dungeon Because I DON’T KNOW!

Currently, Tony can be seen as one of the hosts of popular YouTube engineering program Element14 Presents. He is training to be ProTools certified in both music and audio post-production, and in his spare time enjoys singing, video games, electronics and listening to his favorite band, TOKIO.
