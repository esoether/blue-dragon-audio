Games Music

World War Toons

For this project, I served as audio director, overseeing all aspects of audio production and direction.

In addition to creating every sound effect in the game, I was tasked with creating realistic soundscapes for use in VR, using both sound libraries and field recording techniques. As a title able to be played in both VR and non-VR versions, special care was taken to the creation of environmental sounds so as to still sound compelling in differing stero fields.

Other duties included voice casting, voice directing, script writing, additional music composing, and providing additional voice talent. I was also granted the incredible opportunity to arrange music from the popular Metal Slug franchise for use in an upcoming collaboration! (some of which can be heard here)

Skullgirls 2nd Encore

"Hear Skullgirls' story as never before, try your hand at all new trials and challenges, and see how long you can last in Survival! Also unlocks all five additional characters: sing zombie opera with Squigly, and make beautiful music with Big Band! Unleash Eliza's skeletal Parasite, and howl at the moon with Beowulf! And, finally, learn why humans cry with Robo-Fortune. All while taking advantage of the full range of color options so you'll look spiffy in your next match! It's everything you want, in one convenient package!"

For this project, I assisted in the editing, processing and delivery of voice over assets for the story mode and newly introduced characters.

Regular Show: Mordecai & Rigby in 8-bit Land

"Regular Show: Mordecai & Rigby in 8-bit Land is the very first video game based on the hit Cartoon Network TV Show. Mordecai and Rigby are trapped inside a video game and must travel through the pixilated land of 8-Bit to find their way out. The hijinx-filled adventure is packed with platforming, space shooting, and top down shooter gameplay, and comes fully loaded with tons of Regular Show characters and jokes."

For this project, I served as audio lead, designing all sound effects and overseeing the sound direction across the various gameplay modes featured in this game.

In addition to realistic soundscapes, I am also capable of capturing that retro sound many gamers still very much love! I designed all sound effects for this game (including the WayForward jingle) using a Nintendo Game Boy and Little Sound DJ (LSDJ) software.

Adventure Time Explore the Dungeon Because I DON'T KNOW!

"Princess Bubblegum summons Finn and Jake to the Candy Kingdom to investigate a rash of thefts and kidnappings that have plagued her kingdom. She thinks the cause lies within her Secret Royal Dungeon, where only the nastiest monsters and criminals are locked up. If they’ve found a way to escape, the Land of Ooo will be in danger! Following the mathematical, algebraic, and radical success of 2012’s Adventure Time: Hey Ice King! Why’d You Steal Our Garbage?!!, show creator Pendleton Ward teams up with developer WayForward in the all-new action game Adventure Time: Explore the Dungeon Because I DON’T KNOW!, and team up with your friends in four-player co-operative multiplayer as the Adventure Time world comes to life through beautiful pixel animations, and voiceover from the original cast."

For this project, I provided sound design, as well as voice over editing and additional voice over recording.

This game provided a unique opportunity to create a sound closely in the spirit and style of the hit TV show, while also combining influences from popular dungeon crawlers of the 80s and 90s. This resulted in a variety of colorful and expressive sounds which bring the many different dungeonscapes to life!

The Smurfs 2

"The Smurfs 2 is one of the summer’s top animated family movies and is now a hit video game too! In The Smurfs 2 video game, the evil wizard Gargamel has created the Naughties, and together they travel to Smurf Village, kidnap Smurfette, and imprison her in Paris. It’s now up to Papa Smurf and friends to travel from the Enchanted Forest, through the streets of New York, into Paris, and everywhere in between to save the day and bring Smurfette home safe!"

For this project, I provided additional sound design and voice localization assistance. I also provided the voice of Clockwork Smurf for the Wii U version of the game!

DuckTales Remastered

"DuckTales Remastered is a hand-crafted, beautiful re-imagining of one of the most cherished 8-bit titles of all time. Go back to the golden age of gaming, now refined with a level of detail that will please the most hardened devoted Disney or retro fan alike. Featuring hand-drawn animated sprites, authentic Disney character voices and richly painted level backgrounds from the classic cartoon TV series, Scrooge and family come to life like never before. The gameplay retains the authenticity of its 8-bit predecessor, simple and fun, with slight modifications to improve gameplay and design. Embark on an authentic DuckTales adventure, as Scrooge McDuck and his three nephews; Huey, Dewey and Louie travel to exotic locations throughout the world in their quest to retrieve the five Legendary Treasures."

For this project, I provided sound design as well as voice over editing. This game provided the unique opportunity to take the sound of a game many gamers grew up with and bring it to the modern era!
