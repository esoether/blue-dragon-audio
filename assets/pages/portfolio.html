World War Toons

– Studio Roqovan

For this project, I served as audio director, overseeing all aspects of audio production and direction.

In addition to creating every sound effect in the game, I was tasked with creating realistic soundscapes for use in VR, using both sound libraries and field recording techniques. As a title able to be played in both VR and non-VR versions, special care was taken to the creation of environmental sounds so as to still sound compelling in differing stereo fields.

Skull Girls 2nd Encore

– Lab Zero Games

"Hear Skullgirls' story as never before! Unlock all five additional characters: sing zombie opera with Squigly, and make beautiful music with Big Band! Unleash Eliza's skeletal Parasite, & howl at the moon with Beowulf! And, finally, learn why humans cry with Robo-Fortune. Skullgirls' excellent cast of voice actors bring the story to life like never before!"

For this project, I assisted in the editing, processing and delivery of voice over assets for the story mode and newly introduced characters.

Regular Show: Mordecai & Rigby in 8-bit Land

– WayForward Technologies

For this project, I served as audio lead, designing all sound effects and overseeing the sound direction across the various gameplay modes featured in this game.

In addition to realistic soundscapes, I am also capable of capturing that retro sound many gamers still very much love! I designed all sound effects for this game (including the WayForward jingle) using a Nintendo Game Boy and Little Sound DJ (LSDJ) software.

Adventure Time: Explore the Dungeon Because I DON'T KNOW!

– WayForward Technologies

For this project, I provided sound design, as well as voice over editing and additional voice over recording.

This game provided a unique opportunity to create a sound closely in the spirit and style of the hit TV show, while also combining influences from popular dungeon crawlers of the 80s and 90s.

This resulted in a variety of colorful and expressive sounds which bring the many different dungeonscapes to life!
